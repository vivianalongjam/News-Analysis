About

Our project involves the use of News API, a website from where we call for the latest articles from Indian news sources.

Then, a Word Cloud is formed based on the current news and headlines. By calling on NLP Cloud, we can do a sentiment analysis to see how positive or negative the news is.

Finally, this information is presented on this website.
